Transfers

From Airport to Ubud:

You can get to Ubud from the airport by:

1. Airport Transfer by Klook
Pre-book an airport transfer using Klook. Click this link

2. Grab
Go to the Grab lounge in the airport to book your ride.

3. Taxi
Metered taxis are available at the airport—simply follow the signs to the designated pickup points.

4. Bus
You can take a public bus from the airport to Ubung Bus Station, then transfer to the Ubud line. You can check the bus schedule at this link

To The Venue:
